Green Tea

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Serving: 1 person

Calories 80 calories

Tools Needed:

  • Large pot
  • Cutting board
  • Knife
  • Wooden spoon
  • Measuring cups

Ingredients:

  • 1 green tea bag or 1 tsp loose leaf green tea
  • 1 cup hot water (around 175°F or 80°C)
  • Honey or lemon (optional)

Instructions:

  • Boil water and let it cool for a few minutes (around 175°F or 80°C).
  • Place the tea bag or loose leaf tea into a cup.
  • Pour hot water over the tea and steep for 2-3 minutes.
  • Remove the tea bag or strain the loose leaf tea.
  • Add honey or lemon to taste if desired.
  • Enjoy your classic green tea!
