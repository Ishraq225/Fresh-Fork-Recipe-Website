Zoodle Pasta

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Serving: 2 person

Calories 115 calories

Tools Needed:

  • Large pot
  • Cutting board
  • Knife
  • Wooden spoon
  • Measuring cups

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions:

  • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  • Add zoodles and cook for 3-5 minutes until tender, stirring occasionally.
  • Season with salt and pepper, then sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  • Toss to combine and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!
