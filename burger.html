Vegan Burger

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Serving: 1 person

Calories 500 calories

Tools Needed:

  • Large pot
  • Cutting board
  • Knife
  • Wooden spoon
  • Measuring cups

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Salt ad pepper to taste
  • Lettuce, tomato,and onion(optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayo(optional)

Instructions:

  • Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  • Form the ground beef into 4 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  • Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  • Add a slice of cheese on top of each patty during the last minute of grilling.
  • Toast the burger buns on the grill until lightly browned.
  • Assemble the burger with your choice of toppings and condiments.
